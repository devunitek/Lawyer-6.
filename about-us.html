Adv. Pravin Palhade

Adv. Pravin Palhade has combined 25 years of experience in law and restorative justice and has been providing clients with legal counsel in the areas of Intellectual Property, Family, Estate Planning, Pertitutional, Civil Rights, Contract, Revenue, Consumer Protection, Arbitration, Administrative, Human Rights, Cyber Crime, Co-Operative Society, and Fisheries Co-Operative Societies since 2000.

Situated in the heart of Mumbai (Maharashtra State), the Presto Law Firm is a group of dedicated attorneys and individuals who are here to counsel you through any legal issue you may face. Being confronted with a legal issue is often stressful and even scary. The legal systems are complicated and intimidating. Our team of knowledgeable and experienced individuals is here to guide you through and translate “Legalese” into English. We provide specialist and technical advice based on commercial experience that does not cost the earth. We seek long-term relationships and understand the importance of proactive, personal service. We are trusted to manage complex, valuable transactions because we know and understand our clients’ objectives. With over two decades of experience, the Presto Law Firm has the tools and knowledge to handle your issue.
